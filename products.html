High-Precision Moulding Services

High-precision moulding services, utilizing advanced technology and high-quality materials. We specialize in custom moulding for a wide range of industries, ensuring consistent product quality, durability, and design accuracy.

New Mould Development

We provide expert mould development services, designing and producing new moulds tailored to meet specific client requirements. Our team ensures that each mould is created with attention to detail, precision, and functionality, supporting efficient and scalable production.

Insert Moulding

Insert moulding at Swasttik involves embedding metal, electronic, or other materials into plastic components during the moulding process. This method enhances the strength and functionality of the parts, offering greater durability for complex applications in industries like automotive, healthcare, and electronics.

Over Moulding

Over moulding is a service where we apply an additional layer of plastic over a pre-existing component, typically to improve grip, protection, or insulation. Swasttik uses this technique to enhance the performance and aesthetics of various products, from handles to electronic housings.

Assembly Operations

Our assembly operations go beyond simple moulding. We provide end-to-end solutions that include the integration of multiple parts into a finished product. Whether it’s adding electronics, labeling, or final testing, Swasttik ensures that all assembly processes are carried out with precision and in line with client specifications.
