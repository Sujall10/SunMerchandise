Our Company

From concept to reality with quality and speed

Founded in 2020, Swasttik Plastic Industries is a contract-based plastic manufacturing company committed to delivering high-quality, precision-moulded components across various industries. With a focus on innovation, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we have steadily built our reputation as a trusted partner for custom plastic solutions.

Specializing in services such as injection moulding, new mould development, insert moulding, over moulding, and assembly operations, Swasttik combines technical expertise with advanced production capabilities. Our mission is to provide clients with tailored, efficient, and cost-effective manufacturing support that meets the highest industry standards.

Driven by quality and continuous improvement, Swasttik Plastic Industries continues to grow, developing long-term relationships with clients and contributing to their success through dependable manufacturing solutions.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, high-performance plastics mold that enable our clients to push the boundaries of what's possible in their industries.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in advanced plastic materials, recognized for our technical excellence and commitment to sustainability.

Our Values

Innovation, Quality, Integrity, and Customer Focus guide everything we do at Swasttik Plastic Industries.
