About Us

Sun Merchandising Services is one of the most versatile buying agents in India providing its services to buyers globally. SMS is an international buying agency catering to Textile Garments & Apparels (Men, Women and Kids apparel), Home furnishings, Hand Bags and fashion Jewelry.

We specialize in sourcing, sampling, quality checks and shipment co-ordination Sun Merchandising Services was established in the year 2003, driven by strong values and ethics the company believes that “CUSTOMER COMES FIRST” with a constant endeavor to provide a very high degree of satisfaction to our ever increasing list of discerning global clients.

We believe in providing our clients with unparallel levels of service which are focused on building long lasting relationships by providing superior quality service and high customer satisfaction. Sun Merchandising Services represents Wholesalers, Importers and Retailers from U.S.A, Canada and Europe who source a range of quality garments and other textile products.

We work on very competitive prices to meet the needs of the buyersand are a collaborative partner to international companies for managing sourcing of various ranges of products from India. We are the most trusted textile sourcing agent that have been in liaison with a number of established manufacturers based in Tirupur, New Delhi, Gurgaon, Faridabad, Noida, Ludhiana, Mumbai, Bangalore & Chennai in India.

We invite you to explore this world of collaborative & managed sourcing, which is prepared for demanding customers who settle for nothing but the best.
